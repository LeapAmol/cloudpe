Frequently Asked Questions

What operating systems can I install on my CloudPe virtual machine?
We offer a wide OS library including Ubuntu, CentOS, Debian, RHEL, Windows Server, and more — all available as one-click install templates.
Is there a free trial available, and what are its limitations?
Yes, we offer a 15-day free trial with limited resources so you can explore CloudPe. No credit card is required to get started.
How is CloudPe different from AWS, Azure, or DigitalOcean?
CloudPe offers high-performance infrastructure with up to 70% lower costs, built on OpenStack with local Indian datacenters, 24/7 human support, and transparent billing.
Can I scale my VM resources later (CPU, RAM, storage)?
Absolutely. CloudPe allows you to scale up or down your virtual machine’s configuration at any time via the dashboard or API.
Can I deploy Kubernetes or GPU-powered workloads on CloudPe?
Yes. In addition to VM hosting, we support managed Kubernetes clusters and GPU instances for AI, ML, and rendering workloads
